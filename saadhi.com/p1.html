Virat & Anuskha

We came across our profiles on Shaadi in April 2022, and thought we could be an exact match and connected with each other. Finally our families met and we got married on 9th Oct 2022. Shaadi has been a great facilitator for us to connect and find each other. Thank you Shaadi.com for helping us to get the love for life.
